Project Process

Our standard project workflow includes:

  • Discovery call: Initial consultation to understand your needs and goals

Payment Terms

Final payment: Remaining 50% is due upon project completion and before the website is published live.

Care plans: Monthly care plan fees are billed in advance on the first day of each month via automatic Stripe invoice.

Late Payment Policy

We understand that delays happen. Here is how we handle late payments:

  • 0 to 7 days late: Grace period with friendly payment reminder
  • 8 to 14 days late: £50 late payment fee applied and work paused until payment received
  • 14 or more days late: Statutory interest applies under the Late Payment of Commercial Debts (Interest) Act 1998, currently 8% plus the Bank of England base rate
  • 28 or more days late: Website unpublished and all work stopped until full payment received

Late payment fees and interest will be added to the outstanding balance.
